BIO

I'm a third year Ph.D student supervised by Prof.Siwei Ma and Prof.Wen Gao in the Department of Computer Science at Peking University. I received my B.S degree in Computer Science from Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications (BUPT). I used to work as a research assistant at Institute of Computational Linguistic with Prof.Xu Sun at Peking University on Chinese word segmentation and word embedding.

My research mainly focuses on light field image coding and machine learning based approach and I'm a member of Video Coding Group (VCG), NELVT, Peking University. I am the recipient of the Best Paper Award of the Pacific-Rim Conference on Multimedia (PCM) 2017 conference.
