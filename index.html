BIO

Currently, I'm a visiting scholar at New York University (NYU), Tandon School of Engineering, working with Professor Yao Wang. I'm a third year Ph.D student supervised by Prof.Siwei Ma and Prof.Wen Gao at the Department of Computer Science in Peking University. I received my B.Eng degree in Computer Science from Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications (BUPT). I used to intern as a research assistant at the Institute of Computational Linguistic with Prof.Xu Sun on Chinese word segmentation and word embedding during my junior undergraduate year.

My research mainly focuses on knowledge based and machine learning based approach for video coding and I'm a member of Video Coding Group (VCG), NELVT, Peking University. I am the recipient of the Best Paper Award of the Pacific-Rim Conference on Multimedia (PCM) 2017 conference. Meanwhile, I am recognized by VCIP 2017 technical program committee as one of the Best Reviewers.
