BIO

Currently, I'm a second year Ph.D student in the Department of Computer Science, Peking University. As a member of Video Coding Lab (VCL), my research mainly falls under the area of Image Processing and Video Compression with an emphasis on light field image compression and deep learning based compression, working with Prof.Siwei Ma and Prof.Wen Gao.

Before joining Peking University in fall, 2015, I received my Bachelor's degree in Computer Science from Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications (BUPT). During my junior undergraduate year, I worked as a research assistant at Institute of Computational Linguistic with Prof.Xu Sun in Peking University, with a focus on deep learning application on Chinese word segmentation and word embedding.
