BIO

Currently, I'm a final year Ph.D student supervised by Prof. Siwei Ma and Prof. Wen Gao at the department of computer science, Peking University. I received my B.Sc in Computer Science from Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications (BUPT). I was a visiting Ph.D student at Tandon School of Engineering, New York University (NYU), working with Professor Yao Wang at NYU Video Lab.

My research mainly focuses on learning based algorithms for video coding and I'm a member of Video Coding Lab (VCL), NELVT, Peking University. I am the recipient of the Best Paper Award of the 2017 Pacific-Rim Conference on Multimedia (PCM), the Best Paper Award of 2018 IEEE MultiMedia and the Best Student Paper Award of 2019 IEEE International Conference on Multimedia Information Processing and Retrieval (MIPR). Meanwhile, I received the Best Reviewers of VCIP 2017 and Outstanding Reviewer (both 2016 and 2017) of ELSEVIER Journal of Visual Communication and Image Representation.
