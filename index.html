BIO

Currently, I'm a second year Ph.D student in the Department of Computer Science, Peking University. Before joining Peking University in fall, 2015, I received my Bachelor's degree in Computer Science from Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications (BUPT). I used to work as a research assistant at Institute of Computational Linguistic with Prof.Xu Sun in Peking University, with machine learning algorithms on Chinese word segmentation and word embedding.

Research
As a member of Video Coding Group (VCG), National Engineering Lab for Video Technology (NELVT), my research mainly focuses on Image Processing and Video Compression with an emphasis on light field image compression and machine learning based compression, working with Prof.Siwei Ma and Prof.Wen Gao.
