BIO

Currently, I'm a visiting Ph.D student at New York University (NYU), Tandon School of Engineering, working with Professor Yao Wang at NYU Video Lab. I'm a fourth year Ph.D student supervised by Prof.Siwei Ma and Prof.Wen Gao at the Department of Computer Science in Peking University. I received my B.Sc in Computer Science from Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications (BUPT). I worked to intern at the Institute of Computational Linguistic with Prof.Xu Sun on natural language processing during my junior undergraduate year.

My research mainly focuses on learning based algorithms for video coding and I'm a member of Video Coding Group (VCG), NELVT, Peking University. I am the recipient of the Best Paper Award of the Pacific-Rim Conference on Multimedia (PCM) 2017 and Best Paper Award of 2018 IEEE MultiMedia. Meanwhile, I received the Best Reviewers of VCIP 2017 and Outstanding Reviewer (both 2016 and 2017) of ELSEVIER Journal of Visual Communication and Image Representation.
