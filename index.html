BIO

I'm currently working as Boya Postdoctoral Research Fellow at School of Computer Science, Peking University with Prof. Wen Gao. I received Ph.D from school of EECS, Peking University, supervised by Prof. Siwei Ma and B.E in Computer Science from Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications (BUPT). I was a visiting Ph.D student at Tandon School of Engineering, New York University (NYU), working with Professor Yao Wang at NYU Video Lab.

My research topic focuses on learning based algorithms for video coding and I'm a member of Video Coding Lab (VCL), NELVT, Peking University. I am the recipient of the Best Paper Award of the 2017 Pacific-Rim Conference on Multimedia (PCM), the Best Paper Award of 2018 IEEE MultiMedia and the Best Student Paper Award of 2019 IEEE International Conference on Multimedia Information Processing and Retrieval (MIPR). Meanwhile, I received the Best Reviewers of VCIP 2017 and Outstanding Reviewer (both 2016 and 2017) of ELSEVIER Journal of Visual Communication and Image Representation.
